FAQ on Translations

General

Companies often desire to introduce their Company and Trade Leads in many languages as much as possible. Automatic translations might likely reflect incorrectly information and messages that Companies want to deliver to customers. More importantly, users hardly search information in automatic translated languages!
Recognising these disavantages, ECizi.com has developped tools to help Companies to post, manage and especially search information easily and quickly in 11 languages. Translators registered in ECizi.com can provide translation services to Companies

Role of Ecizi.com on translation services provided by Translators registered in Ecizi.com

Ecizi.com is only the platform to connect Company and Translator who will negociate translation fee and conditions by themselves. Requestors (Companies) are responsible to settle fee due to Translators. ECizi.com is not responsible for any disagreement/dispute or unsettlement of translation fee. Supporting tools are also provided to track, follow and manage translation requests

How to post Company Information and Trade Leads in different languages

Before posting Trade Leads in languages, Company Information must have been already posted in these desired languages! Posting Company Information and Trade leads in languages can be carried out by the Companies themselves or by outsourcing translation to translators registered in ECizi.com (view details in section "posting trade leads")

How to outsource translations?

" Please be remember that Trade Leads can be posted only in languages where Company Information has been posted already. To outsource translations, follow steps:
(1) Trade Lead or Company Information is opened (or inputed) in source language
(2) Click botton ""Outsource translation""
(3) Select target language (single selection)
(4) Select appropriate translator
(5) Input requirements/negociation (proposal of translation fee; time to be completed; payment method; message to Translator)
(6) Translator and Requestor would continue to negociate until the request is accepted or denied. If Translator denies the request, Company (Requestor) can send request to an other Translator. If translator accepts the request Company would wait until the translation is completed
(7) When translator completes (finishes) translation, Requestor has to lock (close) translation. As soon as the Translation is closed, Translator can not make any amendment on translation. ECizi Admin will need to approve closed Translation for further public view and usage
(8) Requestor would note on the system payment status of translation fee (paid or not paid yet) to keep track and record"

What information can be translated by outsourcing to translators registered in Ecizi.com

ECizi.com provides supporting tools to track, manage translation outsourcing only for Trade Leads, Company Information but not other information such emails, chats, attachments, files. The outsourcing should be carried out throught email exchanges and it will be recorded, tracked manually by two parties. Companies (Requestors) can also ask Translators to participate chats with partners. However in such cases ECizi.com will not provide supporting tools to track, manage translation outsourcing. Companies (Requestors) and Translators need to manage by themselves for these kinds of translation outsourcing

Can Companies (Requestors) cancel request which has been accepted by Translator but not completed yet?

They can do it as some exceptional cases require cancellation but it is not encouraged as it does not show professionalism. When fee and conditions on translation have been agreed, cancellation should be avoided

Translator agrees that Requestors (Companies) can cancel translation requests which have been accepted. How can it proceed?

As explained in many sections, cancellation of accepted requests should be avoided but if it's necessary, only Requestor can cancel requests. As such if Translator wants to cancel the Request, s/he must ask Requestor to cancel it

How to know whether Translator accepts or refuses Translation Request?

In "My Ecizi Account", Requestor (Company) can view table "Outsourced Translations" which presents status of all requests and translations

How to know whether Translator completes translations?

In "My Ecizi Account", Requestor (Company) can view table "Outsourced Translations" which presents status of all requests and translations

Requestor or Translator want to change conditions that have been agreed (e.g translation fee; time to complete the translation…)?

Only Requestor can update into (Ecizi) System terms and service fee that both Translator and Requestor have been agreed. (Translator can not do the update). Accepting the request, Translator agrees with terms and service fee updated by Requestor. Later amendment on term and service fee should be agreed out of the system and tracked manually by Translator and Requestor

Can Translators cancel translation requests which they already accepted?

Translators cannot cancel requests that they accepted. Only Requestors can cancel requests. Translators can ask Requestors to cancel the request

How can Requestors (Companies) invite Translator to participate chats with their partners?

If chats have been scheduled before, Requestors (Companies) can arrange with Translators to be one line on scheduled time. Chats can be between more than 2 Ecizi members (Company; Partner Translator). If chats are unscheduled, Requestor can invite only translators on line to participate chats

Can one translation request be sent to more than 1 translator at the same time?

One translation request can be sent only to one translator. If the request is refused, the Requestor can send it to other translator

Can Requestor amend the translation?

As soon as Translator completes translation, Requestor can amend it if necessary.

What do Translators need to do when receiving the Translation requests?

Upon receiving the translation request, Translator needs to review request details including service fee proposed by Requestor; required time to complete…. Translator can negotiate with Requestor by giving his/her proposals. Translator should accept the Request only when he/she agrees with conditions/fee updated in the System by Requestor

How to know there is new translation request?

In "My Ecizi account" of Translator, there is a table summarizing status of requests or translations such as new requests waiting for Translator's acceptance or requests accepted to be completed or Translations completed waiting for Requestor's close

How to know the Company (Requestor) accepts the Translation completed by Translator.

As soon as Requestor (Company) closes translation which has been completed by Translator, it means that Requestor agrees with the Translation. Closing will make translation become "Completed, not paid yet; Requestor locked it" or "Completed, paid and closed (locked) already"

Both Requestor and Translator agree to change terms and service fee which have been agreed

As soon as Translators accept requests, it means that they agree with terms and service fee that Requestors inputted in the system. Changes afterward need to be managed manually by Translators and Requestors

What should I do if I want to change information in source language after Translator has accepted my translation request?

In principle, after accepting translation requests, Translators might be translating information. As such, if Requestor want to amend information of source language, he needs to discuss with Translator to cancel the current request and send to the Translator new request with the updated information. Only Requestor can cancel the translation request. Translator can not cancel it

How can translator register as a normal "selling" company in Ecizi.com?

Firstly, they need to create an ECizi account. Select member type as "Translator". Update Company Information in "My ECizi account". Then also update necessary information to "subscribe translation services" in "My ECizi account". Please note that languages used to "subscribe translation services" are only ones in which Company Information have been inputted.

Can Translator provide translation services for information which is not in Ecizi.com?

Yes but it is the business transactions between Translator and Company (Requestor) which will not be tracked and managed in Ecizi.com system

How to provide translation services for more than one pair of language?

Firstly please remember that you can only subscribe translation service for languages in which Company Information has been inputted. Example: if you want to provide translation services for pair of languages English-French, you have to input Company Information in both English and French. You can then subscribe services for translation from English to French and vice versa. Suggest that you describe your services in both English and French

How can Requestor settle the translation fee to Translator

Requestor needs to settle directly translation fee to Translator. Ecizi provides only tools to update and manage payment status to Translators.

Who will be responsible for updating payment status of translation fee?

Requestor (Company) is responsible for updating payment status of translation fee to the system. Translator can not make the update.
